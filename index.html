Welcome to my personal page!

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the university of Luxembourg since September 2022, under the supervision of Professor Giovanni Peccati. Previously, I was a P.h.D student at Université de Rennes 1 (IRMAR) under the supervision of Jürgen Angst Jürgen Angst and Guillaume Poly.

My general topic of research revolves around the study of limit theorems associated to functionnals of random fields, and in particular to geometric functionals related to excursions sets and to the nodal measure.
